PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 5, 2026

OVERVIEW

Screaming Jeff ("the App") is a voice-controlled game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

DATA COLLECTION

Screaming Jeff does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

MICROPHONE ACCESS

The App requires microphone access to detect your voice volume level for gameplay purposes. Audio from the microphone is processed entirely on your device in real time. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

ANALYTICS

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

HIGH SCORES

Your high score is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. It is not transmitted anywhere.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App does not integrate with any third-party services.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App collects no information from anyone.

CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

CONTACT

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at vladislav.semenov@gmail.com.
