SCREAMING JEFF

MONDAY SURVIVAL

Scream into your phone to help Jeff fly his office chair through an endless cubicle nightmare. Voice-controlled retro arcade chaos.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Scream = Jeff flies up
  • Silence = Jeff falls down
  • Hit a pipe = Jeff gets "restructured"
  • Survive = Meetings dodged

FEATURES

  • Voice-controlled gameplay
  • Retro 90s pixel art with CRT scanlines
  • Hilarious office-themed obstacles
  • Career milestone messages
  • Death messages from HR

FAQ

  • The game doesn't respond to my voice. Make sure you allowed microphone access in Settings > Screaming Jeff.
  • Jeff won't fly. Try speaking louder or moving to a quieter environment.
  • Can I play without screaming? Any voice input works — talking, humming, or beatboxing. But screaming is recommended.
